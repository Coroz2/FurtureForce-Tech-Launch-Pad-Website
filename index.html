About Me

My name is Carlos but you can call me Los. I am from Illinois and live about twenty five minutes from Chicago. Growing up I ignited my interest in computer science when I realized you could recreate the game Flappy Bird within a code editor in Khan Academy. Lacking the normal art skills my twin sister has I found this as an opportunity to express myself with a couple lines of text instead of brush and canvas. I continued this passion into my studies where I now find myself majoring in Computer Science and Linguistics at the University of Illinois. At UIUC I am also am the head recruitment chair in my fraternity and a member of ACM SIGMobile Interest Group. Besides computer science I love playing soccer and am an avid supporter of Manchester United. I am also a big movie fan; some of my favorites include: Good Fellas, Star Wars, Fight Club, The Prestige, and many more. I enjoy all of my pass times, but especially when I have other people to enjoy them with. Theres nothing better in the world than enjoying yourself with others and creating memories with them that will last forever.
